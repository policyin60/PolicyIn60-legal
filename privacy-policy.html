Privacy Policy

Effective: February 24, 2026 Last updated: February 24, 2026
Short version: ClipBot does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users or third parties. This tool processes only publicly available government video content and posts to a single owner-operated social media account.

1. What ClipBot Is

ClipBot is a personal automation tool that downloads publicly available C-SPAN congressional hearing footage, generates highlight clips using AI, and publishes them to a single, owner-operated TikTok account. It is not a public-facing application or platform.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

ClipBot does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

— Names, email addresses, or contact information
— IP addresses or device identifiers
— Location data
— Browsing history or behavioral data
— Cookies or tracking data of any kind
— Account credentials or authentication data from viewers

3. Content Sources

All video content processed by ClipBot originates from C-SPAN, which releases its congressional coverage as public domain. No private, copyrighted, or user-submitted content is ever processed. No individuals' personal data is captured or stored during video processing.

4. Third-Party Services

ClipBot publishes content to TikTok. When you view content posted by ClipBot on TikTok, you are subject to TikTok's own privacy policy and data practices. ClipBot has no control over and takes no responsibility for TikTok's data collection.

ClipBot uses the Anthropic Claude API for AI-based content analysis. Only video transcript text (derived from public C-SPAN footage) is sent to the Claude API. No personal data is transmitted.

5. Data Storage

ClipBot temporarily stores video files and transcripts during processing. These files are automatically deleted after content is successfully published. A minimal database log is maintained containing video titles, timestamps, and platform post IDs — no personal information is included in these logs.

6. Children's Privacy

ClipBot does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. As this service does not collect any user data at all, there is no special risk to minors from the operation of this tool.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Because no personal data is collected, changes to this policy will not materially affect any individual's privacy.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the operator via the associated TikTok account.
